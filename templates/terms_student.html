1. Overview

Welcome to Vaanyan Home Tuition! As a student, you can find and connect with qualified home tutors in your area. These terms explain how our platform works and what you need to know.

What We Offer

Access to verified tutors, flexible scheduling, secure payment processing, and quality education at your doorstep or online.

2. Registration

To use our platform, you need to create an account:

  • Provide your real name, email, phone number, and address
  • Select your grade/class and board (CBSE, ICSE, State Board, etc.)
  • Choose subjects you need help with
  • Students under 18 should register with parental consent
  • Keep your login credentials secure and do not share with others

3. Demo Classes

We offer free demo classes so you can evaluate a tutor before committing:

Demo Class Policy

You are entitled to 2 FREE demo classes with any tutor. Demo classes are only available when you plan to take monthly tuition (not for one-time classes).

  • Demo classes are 1 hour each
  • Use demos to evaluate teaching style and compatibility
  • After demos, decide if you want to continue with the tutor
  • Demo classes cannot be misused - they are only for genuine evaluation
  • Taking only demo classes without intention to continue is not allowed

Important

Misusing demo classes (taking demos from multiple tutors without genuine interest) may result in account suspension.

4. Payment Policy

Our payment system is simple and transparent:

Item Details
Billing Basis Teacher's hourly rate × Hours of classes taken
Payment Cycle 25 classes or 1 month (whichever comes first)
Payment Method UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
Payment Process Pay to Vaanyan UPI → Upload screenshot → Verification
Payment Due Within 3 days of cycle completion

How Payment Works:

1

Classes Logged

Your teacher logs each completed class on the platform

2

Cycle Completes

After 25 classes or 1 month, payment becomes due

3

Pay via UPI

Pay the total amount to Vaanyan's UPI ID

4

Upload Proof

Upload payment screenshot on the platform

5

Verification

We verify and your learning continues!

Example Calculation

If your teacher charges ₹400/hour and you take 15 classes (1 hour each) in a month:

Total Payment = ₹400 × 15 = ₹6,000

5. Your Responsibilities

As a student, you agree to:

  • Treat your tutor with respect and courtesy
  • Be punctual for scheduled classes
  • Inform your tutor at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel
  • Provide a suitable learning environment for home tuition
  • Complete homework and assignments given by the tutor
  • Make payments on time as per the payment cycle
  • Give honest feedback about your learning experience
  • Do not contact tutors outside the platform to avoid fees
  • Do not share tutor's personal contact with others

Prohibited Actions

Contacting tutors outside Vaanyan to bypass platform fees is strictly prohibited and will result in account termination.

6. Cancellation Policy

We understand plans can change. Here's our cancellation policy:

  • 24+ hours notice: Cancel free of charge
  • Less than 24 hours: Class may be counted as taken
  • No-show: Class will be counted and billed
  • Teacher cancellation: You'll get a makeup class or credit

Tip

Always communicate with your tutor through the platform chat for any schedule changes.

7. Refund Policy

Our refund policy:

  • You only pay for classes actually taken
  • If you stop mid-cycle, pay only for completed classes
  • Disputes must be raised within 7 days
  • Refunds (if applicable) will be processed within 7-10 days
  • Demo classes are free and non-refundable

Quality Guarantee

Not satisfied with your tutor? Contact us within the first 5 paid classes for a free tutor change.
