1. Overview

Welcome to Vaanyan Home Tuition! As a teacher, you can connect with students in your area and earn by teaching what you love. These terms explain how our platform works for teachers.

Why Join Vaanyan?

Set your own hourly rate, flexible schedule, steady stream of students, secure payments, and focus on teaching while we handle the rest.

2. Registration & Profile

To register as a teacher:

  • Provide your real name, email, phone number, and address
  • Enter your educational qualifications accurately
  • Specify your teaching experience
  • List subjects you can teach
  • Set your hourly rate (you decide your own rate!)
  • Choose teaching modes (Home Tuition, Online, Group)
  • Write a compelling bio to attract students

Pro Tip

A complete and detailed profile attracts more students. Include your teaching philosophy, achievements, and what makes you unique!

3. Your Earnings

You have full control over your earnings:

  • Set Your Rate: You decide your hourly rate (₹200, ₹500, ₹1000 - whatever you want)
  • Flexible Hours: Teach as much or as little as you want
  • Multiple Students: Take on as many students as you can handle
  • Rate Changes: Update your rate anytime from your profile

Earning Potential

If you teach 4 hours/day at ₹500/hr, you can earn up to ₹60,000/month (after commission). Your earnings grow as you build your reputation!

5. Payment Process

Here's how you get paid:

Step Details
1. Log Classes After each class, log it on the platform with date and duration
2. Cycle Completion After 25 classes or 1 month, payment cycle completes
3. Student Pays Student pays the total amount to Vaanyan
4. Verification We verify the payment
5. You Get Paid Your 90% share is transferred to you

Payment Timeline

Once student payment is verified, your earnings are processed within 2-3 business days.

Important: Log Every Class!

Always log your classes accurately. Unlogged classes cannot be billed, and you won't receive payment for them.

6. Your Responsibilities

As a teacher on Vaanyan, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information about your qualifications
  • Maintain professional conduct with all students
  • Be punctual for scheduled classes
  • Deliver quality education as promised
  • Log all completed classes accurately on the platform
  • Communicate schedule changes in advance
  • Respond to student messages within 24 hours
  • Maintain confidentiality of student information
  • Not share inappropriate content with students
  • Follow all local laws and regulations

Strictly Prohibited

Contacting students outside Vaanyan to bypass platform fees is strictly prohibited. This will result in immediate account termination and forfeiture of pending earnings.

7. Demo Classes

About demo classes:

  • Students are entitled to 2 free demo classes
  • Demo classes are your opportunity to showcase your teaching
  • Give your best during demos to convert students
  • Demo classes are not paid, but lead to paid students
  • Demo classes only apply for monthly arrangements

Convert Demos to Students

A great demo class often leads to months of paid tuition. Put your best foot forward!

8. Account Termination

Regarding account termination:

  • By You: You can leave anytime after completing ongoing commitments
  • By Us: We may terminate accounts that violate our terms
  • Pending Earnings: All verified earnings will be paid even after termination
  • Student Transition: Inform your students about the change

Grounds for Termination

  • Fake qualifications or information
  • Bypassing platform to avoid fees
  • Inappropriate behavior with students
  • Consistent poor quality or complaints
  • Not logging classes accurately
